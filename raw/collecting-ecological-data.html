Overview

Collecting ecological data can come in many forms. At its most basic, we want to write down observations that we believe will help support our ultimate research goal. There are some questions we need to answer to be able to get started though!

  • What types of observations can we make? 
  • What sort of data will support our research goal and why?
  • How do we decide how to collect this data?

With answers to the above questions in hand, we can further explore the tools and process we will use to conduct this research.
