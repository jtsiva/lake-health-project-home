Reagents

The ammonia testing kit includes specialized reagents that react with ammonia in water samples to produce a color change that can be measured by the colorimeter. These reagents are specifically formulated for the medium range (0.00 to 3.00 ppm) ammonia testing.

Proper handling of reagents is important:

  • Store reagents in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Keep bottles tightly sealed when not in use to prevent contamination
  • Follow manufacturer guidelines for shelf life and replacement
  • Use appropriate safety gear when handling chemicals
