About the Project

The Lake Health Project was initiated after our community experienced unprecedented algae growth. Our local scientist, Julianne Taylor, is conducting ecological monitoring to understand the lake's health and develop sustainable solutions.

Over three years, we're measuring:

  • Water quality parameters (temperature, pH, ORP, dissolved oxygen, phosphorus, nitrogen)
  • Vegetation coverage and diversity
  • Wildlife usage through camera traps and surveys

Our goal is to limit algal growth through mechanical weed removal rather than chemical treatments, creating a healthier ecosystem for all.
